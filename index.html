My journey started in graphic design — composition, typography, visual balance. Those principles now drive everything I do in motion.

Over the years I've worked across the full pipeline — from scripting and shoot setup to post-production and final delivery. That end-to-end understanding means I approach every project strategically, not just technically.

Currently at Vinove Software & Services, building explainer videos and product demos that make complex software feel human and desirable.

I believe great motion design is invisible — the audience feels it before they see it.
