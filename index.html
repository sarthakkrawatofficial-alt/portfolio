My journey started in graphic design — composition, typography, visual systems. Those principles didn't stay in design. They became the foundation of everything I do in motion.

Most editors work with footage. I work with intent. I've been across the full pipeline — scripting, shoot setup, lighting, post-production, final delivery — which means I catch problems before they become problems, and I push ideas before the client knows to ask for more.

Currently at Vinove Software & Services, turning complex software into motion that actually connects with people. Before that, I ran creative for 7 brands simultaneously at Digiron — building visual identities from scratch, not just executing briefs.

I believe the best motion design is invisible. The audience feels the emotion before they notice the craft. That's the standard I work to.
